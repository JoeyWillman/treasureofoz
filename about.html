About Treasures of Oz

Treasures of Oz is a partnership that promotes environmental awareness, education, and stewardship across Ozaukee County.

  • Help residents and visitors discover local natural areas
  • Foster awareness of our natural surroundings
  • Encourage meaningful, sustainable engagement
  • Provide a vehicle for ongoing collaboration

About This Site

This interactive site was created by Joseph Willman, a freelance cartographer and GIS specialist, as an independent project inspired by the Treasures of Oz website. While not affiliated with the Treasures of Oz, this project was developed to showcase the abundant natural areas of Ozaukee County.

Learn more about Joseph and his work here: Joseph Willman Portfolio.

How to Use the Map

  • Use the search and filters to find places by type, activity, and location.
  • Click a list item or map marker to open details and photos.
  • Trail lines are shown where data is available.
  • On phones, filters open in a compact panel so the map stays visible.
